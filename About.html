About me

Hi, I'm Alessandra

I’m a multidisciplinary designer with a visual thinking style and curiosity for many different things and topics. I’m drawn to making order out of chaos and turning information into something clear and engaging so as to make it understandable for people.
I believe design is not just about interfaces, but about empathy, curiosity, and making people reflect on the systems and stories that surround them.

I’m currently completing my MSc in Digital & Interaction Design at Politecnico di Milano.
